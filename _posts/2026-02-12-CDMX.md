After a few months of bouncing between friends and family all over the world, going back adn forth to Mexico and Uruguay to work out visa situations, and signing a lease, my wife and I have now officially begun settling into Mexico City :)

It's been a brutal process up to this point, but the city is sooo beautiful and I am so happy to finally be settling in. I am still in the process of learning Spanish, so it's a little bit lonely. My wife does her best to translate for me, and plenty of people speak English, but it's not really the same. So I've been spending a lot of time just exploring, going to various churches and museums. Here are some of the pictures I've taken so fars:

Summary I am interested in how systems work and how they fail. Electrical engineering is my focus, following from an abiding interest in systems theory/control theory met with a concrete desire to build systems. At Kyocera-AVX I have been reverse engineering a wide array of products, evaluating prototype components, and researching emerging application areas to assist our product development and market engineering teams.

Garden's alpha is live on <a href="https://garden.sophia-labs.com">Sophia Labs</a>. Garden is a knowledge management and note taking ecosystem similar to Logseq or Roam Research, but built for LLM integration, knowledge graph integration, and agentic workflows. MCP plugins and a variety of other tools have been release to support the alpha. Check it out!
